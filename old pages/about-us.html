We Are SEVO

A non-profit organization dedicated to empowering Sudanese communities through education, health, and economic opportunity — led by the people we serve, driven by transparency, and committed to long-term dignity and resilience.

Our Story

SEVO began as a small circle of Sudanese and diaspora volunteers responding to urgent needs with simple, local solutions. What started as a grassroots effort has grown into a community-led organization rooted in trust and shared responsibility.

We partner with teachers, health workers, and neighborhood leaders to co-design programs that strengthen what already exists: the wisdom, skills, and resilience of our communities. Every step we take is guided by clarity, accountability, and the belief that change endures when it belongs to the people.

Our Guiding Principles

Transparency

We share how decisions are made and how funds are used, so trust can flourish.

Community-Led

Those closest to the challenges lead the solutions; we follow their vision.

Sustainability

We build programs that last—practical, local, and owned by the community.
